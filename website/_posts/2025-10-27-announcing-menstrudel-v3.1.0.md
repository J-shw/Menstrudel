### What's New
* **Manual Language Setting** You can now manually choose your preferred language within the app.
* **Wear OS Splash Screen** Added a splash screen for the Wear OS application.
* **More Translations** Thanks to the community, the app now has better translation support. Currently we have English, French, German and Italian!

---

### Bug Fixes
* Fixed an issue where the Wear OS app's splash screen was not displaying correctly.
* Removed a duplicated translation for "dayCount."
* Updated app dependencies for timezone and local authentication.
