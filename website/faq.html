Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the private, open-source tracker.

General

What makes Menstrudel different?

Most period trackers sell your data or store it in the cloud. Menstrudel is 100% offline and open-source. We don't have accounts, we don't have ads, and we never see your data.

Is it actually free?

Yes. Menstrudel is a community-driven project. There are no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or hidden fees. It is free forever.

Features & Tracking

What can I track in the app?

Menstrudel allows you to log:

  • Period dates and flow intensity
  • Daily symptoms and custom logs
  • Sexual activity
  • Sanitary products (with change reminders)
  • Contraception: Daily pills and LARC (IUD/Implants)
How accurate are the predictions?

Menstrudel uses an on-device algorithm to analyse your historical cycle data. As you log more cycles, the predictions for your next period and fertile window become increasingly precise.

How do sanitary product reminders work?

When you log a sanitary product change, you can set a notification timer. The app will then alert you when it is time for a change, helping you manage your hygiene and health safely throughout the day.

Is my sexual activity data private?

Yes. Like all other data in Menstrudel, logs regarding sexual activity are stored exclusively on your device's local database. We have no way to access this information, and it is never shared with third parties.

Does it work on smartwatches?

Yes! Menstrudel has a dedicated Wear OS companion app so you can log symptoms and check your cycle directly from your wrist.

Privacy & Security

Where is my data stored?

Your data stays on your device. It is stored in a local SQLite database that is never synced to any server. This is why the app works perfectly without an internet connection.

How do I move my data to a new phone?

Since we don't use cloud accounts, you are in control of your data. You can use the Export feature to create a backup file, then Import that file on your new device to restore your history.

What happens to my data if I lose my phone?

Since data is stored only on your device, we recommend using the "Export" feature regularly. Save your backup file to a secure location (like a private USB drive) so you can restore your history to a new device.

Can I lock the app?

Absolutely. You can enable biometric security (Fingerprint or Face ID) within the app settings to ensure your logs remain private even if someone else is using your phone.

Community & Open Source

How can I contribute or report a bug?

Menstrudel is built by the community. You can report bugs, suggest features, or contribute to the code on our GitHub repository.

Can I help translate the app?

Absolutely! We rely on community volunteers to translate Menstrudel into different languages. If you want to help, please reach out via our Crowdin.
