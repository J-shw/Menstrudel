Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Menstrudel?

Menstrudel is a free, open-source, and completely offline period tracking app. It's designed to be a simple, private, and user-friendly way to track your menstrual cycle and pill intake.

Is Menstrudel free to use?

Yes, Menstrudel is 100% free to use. It contains no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Which platforms is Menstrudel available on?

Menstrudel is available for beta testing on Android, Wear OS and iOS.

Features

What can I track with Menstrudel?

You can track your period, flow intensity, and various symptoms. The app also allows you to log your pill intake with support for 21-day, 28-day, or custom pill packs.

How does Menstrudel predict my next cycle?

Menstrudel uses the history of your past cycles to provide an accurate, on-device estimate for your next period. The more cycles you log, the more accurate the predictions become.

Can I set reminders?

Yes, you can set customisable reminders for your period, pill intake, or when to change your tampon.

Privacy and Data

How is my data stored? Where does it go?

Your privacy is the top priority. All your data is stored locally and securely on your device. Menstrudel is a completely offline app, which means your data is never sent to any server and is never seen by anyone but you.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it will be permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.
